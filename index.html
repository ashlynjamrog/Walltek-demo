About Us

Walltek Construction, LLC is based on the belief that our customers' needs are of the utmost importance. We pride ourselves on safely and professionally providing structural earth, gravity block, modular block and mechanically stabalized earth retaining wall installation solutions for our customers. As a result, a high percentage of our business is from repeat customers and referrals.
We would welcome the opportunity to earn your trust and deliver you the best service in the industry.
